# Hurricane Season HVAC Checklist (June)

Prepare your AC system before the first storm threatens.

## Documentation
- [ ] Photograph data plates on indoor and outdoor units
- [ ] Record: Make _____ Model _____ Serial _____
- [ ] Store photos in cloud (Google Drive, iCloud, etc.)
- [ ] Note installation date: _____
- [ ] Confirm homeowner's insurance covers HVAC wind/flood damage
- [ ] Note insurance deductible: $_____

## Outdoor Unit Security
- [ ] Tighten condenser mounting bolts
- [ ] Check that unit pad is level and stable
- [ ] Trim trees/branches within 10 feet of unit
- [ ] Identify loose items nearby that could become projectiles
- [ ] Note flood zone status: Zone _____ (check FEMA maps)
- [ ] If in flood zone: unit elevation above grade: _____ inches

## Shutoff Knowledge
- [ ] Locate and test electrical disconnect (gray box near outdoor unit)
- [ ] Locate AC breaker in main electrical panel (label if not labeled)
- [ ] Know how to safely turn off gas if you have a gas furnace

## Supplies
- [ ] Extra air filters on hand
- [ ] Flashlight with fresh batteries near AC equipment
- [ ] Phone number of HVAC contractor saved in phone: (___)___-____
