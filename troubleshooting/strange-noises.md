# Troubleshooting: Strange AC Noises

Different sounds indicate different problems. Here's what each one means.

## Clicking (Outdoor Unit)
**When:** At startup or shutdown
**Normal?** Brief clicking at startup/shutdown is normal (relay switching).
**Problem if:** Continuous clicking, clicking without the system starting.
**Cause:** Failed capacitor, faulty relay, or control board issue.
**Action:** Call a pro if clicking is continuous or system won't start.

## Buzzing (Outdoor Unit)
**Cause:** Loose parts, debris in unit, failing motor bearings, or loose fan blade.
**Action:** Turn off system. Visually inspect for debris. If clear, call a pro.

## Grinding (Indoor or Outdoor)
**Cause:** Motor bearings failing. Metal-on-metal contact.
**Action:** Turn off system immediately. Running with failed bearings causes further damage. Call a pro.

## Squealing / Screeching (Indoor)
**Cause:** Belt-driven blower motor with worn belt, or direct-drive motor bearings failing.
**Action:** Turn off system. Call a pro. Continuing to run risks motor burnout.

## Rattling (Outdoor)
**Cause:** Loose screws, bolts, or panels. Debris inside the unit. Loose fan blade.
**Action:** Power off, inspect visually. Tighten obvious loose parts. Remove visible debris. If rattling persists, call a pro.

## Hissing (Indoor, near refrigerant lines)
**Cause:** Refrigerant leak. Gas escaping under pressure.
**Action:** Call a pro immediately. Don't try to locate or fix a refrigerant leak yourself.

## Banging (Outdoor, compressor area)
**Cause:** Loose or broken component inside the compressor. Compressor mounts failing.
**Action:** Turn off system. This usually means the compressor is failing. Call a pro.

## Gurgling / Bubbling (Indoor)
**Cause:** Air in the refrigerant line (indicating a leak), or condensate drain line issue.
**Action:** If accompanied by poor cooling, likely a refrigerant issue. Call a pro.

## Whistling (Supply vents)
**Cause:** Airflow restriction — closed or blocked vents, undersized ducts, dirty filter.
**Action:** Open all vents, check and replace filter, ensure no furniture blocks vents.
