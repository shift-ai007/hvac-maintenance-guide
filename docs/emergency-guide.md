# HVAC Emergency Troubleshooting Guide

## AC Not Cooling (Summer)

### Quick Checks (5 min)
1. Thermostat set to COOL, temp below room temp
2. Air filter clean/replaced
3. Circuit breaker not tripped
4. Outdoor unit running, no ice buildup
5. All vents open and unblocked

### Call a Pro When
- Refrigerant leak (hissing, ice on copper lines)
- Compressor clicking but not running
- Burning smell from vents
- Water leaking from indoor unit
- System short-cycling (on/off every few minutes)

## Strange Noises

| Noise | Likely Cause | Urgency |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Grinding | Motor bearings | HIGH — turn off |
| Banging | Loose component | MEDIUM |
| Hissing | Refrigerant leak | HIGH |
| Clicking | Relay/electrical | MEDIUM |
| Squealing | Belt/bearing | MEDIUM |

## Air Quality Issues

- Musty smell = mold in ducts → [Duct cleaning](https://ac-repair.today/services/duct-cleaning/)
- Excessive dust = poor filtration → [Indoor air quality](https://ac-repair.today/services/indoor-air-quality/)
- Dry air = humidifier needed → [AC maintenance](https://ac-repair.today/services/ac-maintenance/) includes humidity check

## Prevention Tips

- Change filter every 1-3 months
- Keep outdoor unit clear (2ft clearance)
- Don't close more than 20% of vents
- Schedule seasonal maintenance twice a year
